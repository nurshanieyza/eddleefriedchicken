Our Story

It's all started since 1999

Welcome to Eddlee Fried Chicken. Starting from 1999 its journey started from small stall in a night market to a big restaurant that has been established 2003 to 2012 it have been serving complete friends and family meals at affordable prices.

Eddlee Fried Chicken is well known as food industries in Malaysian since 2003. The company was founded by Mr. Mohammad Eddlee Bin Hj. Ismail and his father Mr Hj Ismail Bin Mohamed. They both were team up together to create food products. The name of Eddlee is based on the founder name Mohammad Eddlee Bin Hj. Ismail. They also serve many interesting menus at their restaurant such as Fried Chicken (Original), French Fries, Chicken Roll, Mushroom Soup, Chicken Chop and so on.

Our Goals

Eddlee Fried Chicken expect to grow its reputation and business in future and hope the marketing plan will contribute in reaping the root towards a successful food chain in its brighter future. Eddlee Fried Chicken will expand its presence to other states in Malaysia, increasing the number of branches and customer base, thereby strengthening our market position. We will introduce new and exciting menu items to cater to the evolving tastes and preferences of our customers, ensuring that our offerings remain fresh and appealing.

Our focus will be on improving customer retention through enhanced customer service and loyalty programs, fostering a loyal customer base that will drive repeat business and positive word-of-mouth. Eddlee Fried Chicken is dedicated to ensuring the highest standards of food safety and quality, guaranteeing that every meal served meets the highest standards of taste, freshness, and hygiene. Our goal is to consistently deliver exceptional service to our customers, ensuring that every visit to our restaurant is a memorable and satisfying experience.
